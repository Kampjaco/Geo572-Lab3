Fantasy Animation Map

JACOB KAMPF

This map style was based off of visual elements from different animated fantasy movies and TV shows I have enjoyed watching recently.

The map makes use of the color schemes, textures, and font styles from these media.

The main color scheme is from the Spiderman - Across the Spiderverse inspiration piece. The three main colors: black, red and blue, coinside with the Spidersuit Miles Morales wears.

The pink building color is a nod to the character Gwen Stacy and the Spidersuit she wears.

The water pattern is the background from the still from Puss in Boots 2. Most of the action scenes in this movie use 2D animation with a sketched/pencil drawing background, which makes it asthetically pleasing to look at. A part of this sketch pattern is displayed in the water of the map.

Finally, the font used for all labels on the map is the font from the title of the Arcane inspiration piece.
